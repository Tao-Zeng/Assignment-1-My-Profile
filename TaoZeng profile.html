Project Idea


People often say that the advancement of science and technology will bring more demand and will bring new industries, and even subvert the existing order. For example, benefiting from the demand brought by the Internet, Amazon overturned the traditional business model. Short videos on mobile phones have replaced ordinary people’s entertainment, thanks to the faster internet speed brought by 4G. I think the future demand should be focused on “AR". This is cutting-edge technology, and there are still many problems that have not been overcome. AR still has a lot of room for development.

At present, many technology companies have begun to focus on the implementation of AR technology, and have begun to implement a small part of the application. For example, Apple has also realized the application of AR on mobile phones in the early years（https://www.apple.com/augmented-reality/） And there are many rumors that Apple is developing AR glasses and launching them in the future. At present, this has not been confirmed by Apple, But from a business perspective, the Digi-Capital report shows that AR/VR investment in 2019 was US$1.4 Billion ( https://venturebeat.com/2020/03/12/digi-capital-over-4-1-billion-invested-in-ar-and-vr-in-2019/). So we have reason to believe that in the future, the demand and revolution brought by AR will be very huge.

The current AR application range is very small, only applicable to programs on mobile phones, such as simulating the size of a sofa, etc. But what I want to talk about is a completely new concept. It is an AR system that the user can completely control, more like a virtual computer, but it does not need to take up a very large space, because everything is a virtual projection. For example, if you are an architect, you need to draw a series of drawings before you work. However, with this AR system, you can build the look you want in a virtual way, thereby making the design more three-dimensional, getting rid of a series of tedious drawings and mathematical calculations, and even in AR The damage caused by earthquakes is simulated in the system. To be precise, it is a tool, similar to a computer, but more flexible than a computer. Anytime, anywhere, as long as you wake up the system, you can use it. For example, when a user sees a car on the Internet, but the user does not know that his garage does not have enough space for storage, he only needs to hold the projector After connecting to the system, the system will fully display the car, including the size of the car after the door is opened. This system is not only helpful to life but also vital to a criminal investigation. When some vicious incidents occur, the AR system can also help the police solve the case. The police can use AR to scan the corpse of the deceased, and then the AR system will restore a part of the scene based on the condition of the corpse, such as what happened before falling to the ground. When scanning the criminal’s footprints, you can also restore the criminal’s height, shoe size and other information. This AR system does not limit the venue or environment, as long as your product can be connected to the AR system and has a projection function. But this system needs to be connected to the Internet or mobile network. This is a very powerful system that can be used in almost all kinds of scenarios. For modern society, it can also act as an education platform. It can even project the appearance of the teacher in class, allowing students to simulate the appearance of the school at home. Especially in the face of the current epidemic, it is very helpful. This system can help people's communication costs lower and make human life more convenient. It can scan the user’s own body and appearance and upload it to the database, and then when the user and others have a meeting, they can transmit their own pictures to the other party’s system in real time and put them out, so that people face to face. The way of communication is no longer limited to the camera, no need to stay in front of the camera.

At present, the largest application that can be seen is Apple's mobile phone products, almost all new mobile phones have this function. What I’m talking about is an enhanced version of Apple’s AR technology and independent of mobile phones. AR technology is still in a very early stage, and the required technology is also very much and complicated. In terms of hardware: sensors include 3D cameras or scanners, as well as some wearable devices for motion capture. The foundation of the software includes C++/C#/JS, etc. Scene construction such as game engine UE4/U3D, modeling software MAYA/3DSMAX. There are also some basic principles: graphics/human-computer interaction/artificial intelligence/ergonomics/biology/neurology. At present, it has not yet been achieved, and a great breakthrough is needed in all areas. For example, the implementation of 5G and the reduction of equipment costs, etc.

If this project can be realized in the future, it will be a huge human progress, people's productivity will once again increase, and people's imagination will be realized because of the expansion of AR. It not only includes providing convenience in life, but also allows ideas that were only in everyone's mind before can be displayed on a virtual display through AR and then seek feasibility. In the past, scientists or biochemists had to do a lot of experiments to get the answer, and a lot of resources and time were wasted. Through AR technology, unnecessary waste can be eliminated in the early stage of the experiment, thus moving towards real development. We all look forward to creating a more efficient, safe and reliable social system when AR arrives.
